# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Researchers, fabrication operators, and project editors in the McMaster Quantum Photonic Group who track wafers and dies through laboratory process steps, checkpoints, measurements, and rework.

## Product Purpose

WaferWatch provides one dependable operational record for wafer fabrication. It should make the current process state, responsible person, completed work, required review, and chronological history immediately understandable while keeping every operational change persistent and attributable.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, and trustworthy. The interface should feel like a focused laboratory tool with familiar controls, clear state language, and restrained visual emphasis.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboard patterns, novelty interactions, excessive cards, hidden state changes, and redesigns that replace established process controls with a new workflow metaphor. Do not make color the only indicator of process state.

## Design Principles

- Preserve familiar operational surfaces and add new behavior locally.
- Make process state and the next permitted action explicit.
- Keep the visual process map understandable without using it to invent hidden business rules.
- Prefer chronological, attributable history over inferred or ambiguous status.
- Keep editing responsive while persistence and authorization remain authoritative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard operation, visible focus, semantic controls, reduced-motion preferences, text labels alongside state colors, and touch-safe alternatives for drag interactions.
